📐 Test Cases

👉 Test A: Spring-Loaded Hold (Stateless)
Press and **hold the Shift key** down. Move your cursor around. Outlines and data badges appear. **Release the Shift key**—they disappear instantly. Click while holding Shift to capture.

👉 Test B: Morphing Indicator Toggle (Stateful)
Click the **pulsing float orb** in the bottom-right to open the mode dropdown. Choose **Prompt Mode** to enter inspect mode statefully. Hover elements and click to capture.

👉 Test C: High-Precision Outline & Morphing Transitions
While in inspect mode, hover between the cards. Watch the crisp 1px outline **smoothly slide and morph** to fit the bounding boxes and match rounded corners. Notice the floating badge showing: `tag#id.class [width × height]`.

👉 Test D: Preserving Modes (Home, Prompt, Debug)
Select **Debug Mode** from the orb dropdown to view the live telemetry stream console overlay in the bottom-left. Switch to **Home Mode** to disable all tracking overlays. Learn in the walkthrough how Ripple preserves these states.
